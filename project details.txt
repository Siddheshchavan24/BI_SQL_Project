Sales Insights Dashboard – SQL Side
Scenario:
You work as a BI Intern at a retail company. Your manager has given you a PostgreSQL database with tables like customers, orders, products, and sales. You're asked to derive insights using SQL.

SQL Tasks You’ll Do:
Total sales by city
Top 3 products by revenue
Monthly revenue trends
Customer who placed the highest order
Most selling product category
Use of JOINS, GROUP BY, WINDOW FUNCTIONS, SUBQUERIES
